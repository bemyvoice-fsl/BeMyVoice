ABOUT US

This is a social media page for a Filipino Sign Language Processing Service Website for Persons with Speech and Hearing Impairment. We are Grade 12 Students from the Valenzuela City School of Mathematics and Science based in Valenzuela City, Philippines. Our goal is to bridge the communication between persons with auditory and speech impairments and those with none.
